Recipe #1

Lemon Chicken

  • Prep time: 5 min
  • Cook time: 30 min
  • Calories: 465
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
boneless chicken breasts 4
olive oil 1/4 cup
oregano, dried 2 tsp
thyme, dried 2 tsp
garlic powder 2 tsp
salt, divided 2 tsp
lemon juice, freshly squeezedd 22 tbsp
lemon 1

Step 1: Heat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Step 2: Pat the chicken breasts dry and place them in a 9 x 13 baking dish.

Step 3: In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, oregano, thyme, garlic powder, 1 teaspoon salt and pepper to create a thick marinade/paste. Coat the chicken breast with seasoning paste.

Step 4: Using the same bowl mix together the white wine, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, sugar and remaining 1 teaspoon salt. Pour over chicken breast.

Step 5: If using, nestle the lemon slices between the chicken bake for 15 minutes, baste the chicken with the pan juice, bake for another 15 minutes or until the internal temperature of the chicken reads 165 F.

Recipe #2

Salmon en Papillote

  • Prep time: 10 min
  • Cook time: 12 min
  • Calories: 294
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
salmon, cut into 4 pieces 1.5 lbs
small zucchini, thinly sliced 1
head fennel, thinly sliced 1
lemon, thinly sliced 1
dried dill 1 tsp
Extra virgin olive oil 4 tsp
salt 1 tsp
pepper 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F.

Step 2: Cut parchment into 4 large ovals 15” by 10” inches. And fold in half.

Step 3: On half of each parchment layer on ¼ of the zucchini, ¼ the fennel, 1 piece of salmon, sprinkle with ¼ tsp salt, ¼ pepper, ¼ tsp dill, lemon slices, drizzle each salmon with 1 teaspoon olive oil and a few pieces of fennel fronds. Repeat with the three remaining salmon fillets.

Step 4: Close the parchment by folding the other half over the prepared salmon and carefully roll the open edges toward the center.

Step 5: Set pouches on a baking sheet and bake on the center rack for 15 minutes.

Step 6: Open carefully using a fork and serve.

Recipe #3

Broccoli-pesto-pasta

  • Prep time: 20 min
  • Cook time: 15 min
  • Calories: 623
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
cooked pasta of your choice, pasta water reserved 1 lb
broccoli florets 4 cups
basil leaves, lightly packed 2 cups
garlic cloves 2
garlic powder 2 tsp
pine nuts 1/4 cup
extra virgin olive oil 1 cup
salt 1/2 cup
Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated 1 cup

Step 1: In a large pot bring water ** to a rapid boil.

Step 2: Add the broccoli, bring the water back up to a boil for two minutes. Do not drain the water! Immediately transfer broccoli (with a slotted spoon) to a colander place under cold running water to stop further cooking.

Step 3: Bring the water ** back to a boil and add your favorite pasta and cook according to the package (reserve 1 cup of pasta cooking water before draining).

Step 4: In a large blender or food processor, blend together broccoli, basil, garlic, pine nuts, olive oil, salt and Parmigiano-Reggiano until smooth. Slowly pour in 1 cup of reserved pasta water until a nice sauce is formed. If sauce is too thick, add more pasta water one tablespoon at a time.
